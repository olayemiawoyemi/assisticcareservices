ABOUT THE AGENCY

About Assistic Care Services

Assistic Care Services Inc. is a non-profit health care agency that provides compassionate and professional supports to persons with intellectual and developmental disabilities to become fully integrated into their homes and communities in the District of Columbia and Maryland.

We adopt the philosophy of person-centered thinking at all levels of our services. We deliver quality services, client satisfaction and reliability. Our staffs are outstanding and experienced in service delivery.

OUR PURPOSE

Mission

The Mission of Assistic care Services is to provide quality supportive services with respect and dignity to people living with intellectual and developmental disabilities for them to live a meaningful life and be fully integrated into their community at large.

Our agency provides services that encourages self-advocacy, self-direction, community inclusion, natural support relationships, health & wellness, and also adopting person centered thinking philosophy at all level of service.

Core Offerings

The Services We Offer

A dependable, compassionate presence you can trust.
01

Companion Services

Companion services provide a non-medical assistance and supervision to support a person’s goals, desires and needs, as identified in the person’s Individual Support Plan(ISP) or Plan of care to promote the person’s safety, self independence, community integration, and/or retirement.
02

Host Home

This service enables people to live in the community in a family-type setting that will support them to achieve their goals, participate in community life and activities, maintain their health, and retain or improve skills that are important to them.
03

In-Home Supports

Assistic Care Services Inc. provides In-Home Support Services to persons living in their own home or living in their family member’s home. In-Home Support services entails services that provide habilitative support, personal care, assistance with activities of daily living and other support services to the person in their home.
04

One Time Transitional Services

Quality transitional services assisting individuals smoothly navigate structural, domestic or residential moves, ensuring continuity of standard support, health access, and immediate integration.
05

Residential Habilitation

Residential Habilitation Services is provided in a most integrated home setting, for persons with Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities, serving four(4) to six(6) persons. Services are provided in accordance with each person’s approved Individual Support Plan (ISP) and Plan of Care.
06

Supported Living

Supported Living Services are provided to persons who require specified formal and informal supports in a home and have an assessed need for assistance with acquisition, retention, or improvement of skills related to activities of daily living, personal care and nursing.
07

Respite Care

Respite care services is provided for short-term or temporary relief for parents, caregiver, custodians or those who are caring for family members enrolled in the Home Community Based Services Waiver. Respite care will ensure that persons will continue to receive services and have access to community activities.

FOUNDATIONAL VALUES

Our Core Values

At Assistic Care Services, we handle all our services with the following qualities and principles.

RESPECT

Treating each person with genuine appreciation, acknowledging their voice, preferences, and personal path.

EXCELLENCE

Striving for premium service standards, exceptional program support, and meticulous training of our staff.

ADVOCACY

Promoting self-advocacy and assisting individuals in making independent choices regarding their lifestyle and care.

DIGNITY

Preserving privacy, encouraging natural independence, and respecting personal choices at all stages of care.

INTEGRITY

Operating with absolute transparency, high accountability, and ethical standards across our whole organization.

TEAMWORK

Collaborating closely with family members, professional coordinators, and community elements for a comprehensive solution.
